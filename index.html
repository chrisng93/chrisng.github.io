    Portfolio

    Hypeset

    hypeset is a clothing news and sales aggregator. It parses Grailed and Hypebeast for news and calls the Reddit API for sales on popular brands. I check Grailed, Hypebeast, and the Reddit frugalmalefashion subreddit pretty frequently and decided I might as well have something check them for me.

    Technologies

    React     Redux     Thunk     Node.js     Express
    PostgreSQL     Redis

    Dished.

    dished. is an app designed to help you figure out where to eat. You give it your location, how far you want to travel, how you want to travel (car, bike, public transit), and the food you\'re feeling, and it'll tell you the best places around your area. Inspired by a love for food, people\'s indecision when it comes to choosing a restaurant, and a desire to try out new technologies (Flask and Redux-Observables).

    Technologies

    React     Redux     Redux-Observables
    Flask     SQLite     Redis

    Fitomo

    Fitomo is a gamification fitness app that allows you to compete with friends and level up your character. It connects to the Fitbit and Jawbone (RIP) APIs and uses your data to allow you to gain XP for your character. This was created with some classmates at Hack Reactor.

    Technologies

    React     Redux     Node.js     Express
    Flask     MySQL

    Batcher

    Batcher is a Soundcloud batch downloader. It grabs a playlist from Soundcloud, lets you choose some metadata regarding songs\' mp3 files (song title and artist), and then allows you to download the songs in that playlist. This one was inspired by my love for music and laziness in downloading it.

    Technologies

    React     Redux     Thunk     Node.js
    Express     CasperJS
